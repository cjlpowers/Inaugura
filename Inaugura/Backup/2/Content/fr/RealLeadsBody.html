RealLeads: Real Estate Call Management System

Inaugura's RealLeads is your opportunity to broadcast real time listing information via the telephone efficiently and conveniently. Potential buyers call a toll free number listed at the property. This number gives them the option to listen to property information, directly connect to your programmed contact number of choice, or to simply leave a message. This solution requires no hardware or additional facilities at your end. Inaugura's state of the art Datacentre handles every call, keeping your daily tasks on track.
