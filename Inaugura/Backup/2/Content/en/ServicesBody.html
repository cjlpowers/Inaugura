RealLeads Call Management

The RealLeads call management system was designed with both the vendor and seller in mind.  All calls are processed at our central station therefore, liberating this task away from you the vendor.  This system features a property description, open house information and a real-time follow me service with full featured voicemail options. 

The settings can be edited via the web and telephone and changes are done instantly.  Potential clients simply call a local number and enter your property number, they are then asked to choose simple options to receive information regarding your property or are simply transferred to your numbers of choice.  When they are transferred you have the option to accept the call or simply send it to our fully featured voicemail system.

This system eliminates important missed calls and offers you the flexibility to be reached when and where you want.
