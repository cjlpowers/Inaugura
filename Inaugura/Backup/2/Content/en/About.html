Founded in 2005 as a continuation to a UW Enterprise Co-op Initiative, Rentleads is a service for people to post and view rentals nationwide through a an easy to use web interface.

With Rentleads,

Owners (Lease Holders) can:

  • Create listings that may be viewed by renters nation wide.
  • Change the status of listings to reflect availability.
  • Communicate with interested renters.

Renters can:

  • Search for listings matching specific criteria, including proximity to Universities and other landmarks.
  • Communicate with listings owners.
