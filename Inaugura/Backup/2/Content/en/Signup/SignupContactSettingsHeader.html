6. Contact Settings

In this step you will set when and where interested parties will be able to contact you.

Phone Number: This is the number where you can be contacted.

Start Time: This is the earliest time you wish to receive calls about your property.

Stop Time: This is the latest time you wish to receive calls about your property.

Days: The days of the week which you wish to receive calls.

Note: You can specify additional contact schedules by loggin into the RealLeads site following this sign up process.
