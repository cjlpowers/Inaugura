FAQ

How do I signup for the Realleads service?
Simply go to the trial signup section located in the sellers menu and follow our simple setup process.
 
What is the free trial?
The free trial is a seven day period where you can configure your listing on our service.  We allow the free trial to ensure your complete satisfaction before activating your public listing.
 
How do I activate my listing so that the public can see and hear detailed information about my property?
To activate your account, login to the realleads website and choose the activate listing link located under the my listing menu. You must then click on the buy now button (Paypal) and follow the payment steps.
 
What payment methods do you accept?
Realleads accepts all payment options allowed by Paypal.  We chose this solution to ensure the security of your payments.
 
How do I setup my prompts for the call management system?
To access the configuration menu for the call management system, simply call the RealLeads local number, enter your listing number and press * at the main greeting.
 
How do I change my property description?
To edit your property description, login to the realleads website and choose the view listing option under my listing.  You will the have the option to change any description you wish.
 
How do I add/edit/delete pictures on my web listing?
To configure the pictures for your web listing, login into the RealLeads site and choose the images link located in the My Listing section. 
 
How do I setup or change my call management contact schedule?
Login to the RealLeads website and choose contact settings under my listing.
  
How do I check my voicemail?
To check your voicemail, you have 2 options:
Simply logon to the RealLeads website and click on the voicemail link located under the My Listing section
  1. Call into the RealLeads Call Management Service, enter your property number, press * during the main prompt and choose the voicemail option.  The prompts will guide you through this procedure. 
  2. Simply logon to the RealLeads website and click on the voicemail link located under the My Listing section
How do I check my account status and caller information, graphs etc...?
To check your account status, logon to thr RealLeads website and click the Overview link located under the My Listing section.  In this section you will be able to check mail, configure your contact schedule and see the traffic your listing has created.
